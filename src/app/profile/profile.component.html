About me:

He is a Romanian professional footballer who plays mainly as an attacking midfielder for Russian club Ural Yekaterinburg. He began his senior career in Romania with Fink Fenster Petrești in the third league, going on to amass over 100 Liga I appearances for Jiul Petroșani, Steaua București and Gloria Buzău combined. He then changed countries for the first time in 2012, and has since competed professionally in Ukraine, China and Russia. Bicfalvi has been a full Romania international since November 2014.
